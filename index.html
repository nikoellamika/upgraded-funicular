#1

Haze fell deeper and deeper into madness as she held the mirror in front of herself -- she was gone. All her features, her hair, her face, her clothes. The only thing she could see was a pitch black silhouette that signified her presence but nothing, besides the room the surrounded her, made sure that she was the person that she saw in the reflection. Maybe she's looking into another universe somehow. It can't be her. So many people face themselves like this but not her!
As horror made her shudder, she threw the mirror to the other side of the room and it fell on the floor and broke. She didn't leave her room that week.
